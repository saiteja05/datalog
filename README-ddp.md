## üí° Presenter Tips

### Before the Demo
- [ ] Know your audience level (executive vs technical)
- [ ] Have specific use cases ready for their industry
- [ ] Prepare pricing discussion points if relevant
- [ ] Test the page loads smoothly

### During the Demo
- **Adapt to interests** - skip sections if they're engaged elsewhere
- **Use the navigation** - don't force linear scrolling
- **Point out interactive elements** - hover effects on cards
- **Connect to their needs** - "For your use case, this means..."

### Common Questions

**Q: How does Atlas pricing work?**
A: Pay for what you use - compute, storage, data transfer. Free tier available for development.

**Q: Can we use our existing MongoDB skills?**
A: Yes, same query language, drivers, and tools. Atlas adds managed infrastructure.

**Q: What about compliance (HIPAA, SOC2, etc.)?**
A: Atlas is certified for major compliance frameworks. Dedicated clusters available for regulated workloads.

**Q: How does multi-cloud work?**
A: Deploy clusters across AWS, Azure, and GCP simultaneously. Data replicates automatically.

**Q: What if we need to leave Atlas?**
A: Your data is always yours. Export anytime. No proprietary lock-in.
