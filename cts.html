Core Technology Capabilities

These three features of MongoDB Atlas Vector Search are the foundation of every Sentinel application.

Semantic Search

Finds data based on conceptual meaning, not just keywords. The core of understanding user intent.

Hybrid Search

Combines semantic search with precise metadata filtering (e.g., by date, client, or project) for unparalleled accuracy.

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)

Grounds generative AI answers in your own trusted data, preventing "hallucinations" and ensuring factual, verifiable responses.
